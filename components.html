Navigation Menu

Menu navigation will be a horizontal bar located at the top of the page. It will be left aligned, and include the home button, login, as well as a dropdown for Volunteer info such as becoming a volunteer, posting events to volunter, looking for an organization, or checking your events. To the right will be the search box, along witht the search button to execute the search a user is looking for. An example is located below.
